“Trusted Hands” is the NGO located in Agartala, Tripura.

A small step to build greener environment

This Non-profit organization starts its first work on 5th June 2022. On this day to maintain a greener environment, this organization planted over 50 seedlings. For this activity we took permission from the sub divisional forest ranger officer regarding this initiative.
